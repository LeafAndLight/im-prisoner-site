I Have a Plan

A high-energy co-op VR prison escape room. Choose a route, outsmart cameras, crack passwords, disable security systems, and escape together—then earn a score based on time, loot, and teamwork.

Escape the prison. Together.

You and your friends are locked inside a maximum-security prison. In the lobby, you can chat, watch live runs, pick a class, and prepare for the next breakout.

  • Lobby hub: voice chat, class selection (Tank / Stealth / Engineer), waiting area, and mini-games (planned).
  • Dynamic prison: every run can reveal different weak points and new opportunities to break through.
  • Stealth + puzzles: avoid cameras, disable lights & signals, unlock doors, and shut down surveillance.
  • Score system: time, items collected, money saved, and how many prisoners escape together.

Platforms: Meta Quest 2 / Quest 3 / Quest 3S. PC VR support is planned if possible.

Choose your route

Three escape paths—each with its own risks, difficulty, and rewards. Routes can connect at certain points.

  • Sky Escape — high climbs, risky shortcuts, big payoff.
  • Road Escape — timing, coordination, bold plays.
  • Underground Escape — dark tunnels, hidden passages, dangerous detours.

Competitive co-op

  • Lobby board: best scores achieved in the room.
  • Global board: world-wide rankings.
  • Funny moments: physics + teamwork = chaos.
  • Consequences: every action affects the escape.
